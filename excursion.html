Wild Rove Safaris Excursions

Nairobi National Park

Experience a unique safari within the city limits at Nairobi National Park. Witness wildlife such as lions, giraffes, zebras, and rhinos against the backdrop of the city skyline.


David Sheldrick Wildlife Trust

Visit the elephant orphanage at the David Sheldrick Wildlife Trust, where you can interact with and adopt orphaned elephants. Learn about conservation efforts for these majestic animals.

The
Giraffe Centre

Get up close and personal with endangered Rothschild's
giraffes at the Giraffe Centre.
Feed these gentle
giants and learn
about giraffe
conservation.

Karen Blixen Museum

Explore the former home of the famous Danish author Karen Blixen,
best known for "Out of Africa." The museum offers a glimpse into her life and times.

Karura Forest

Escape the hustle and bustle of the city with
a visit to Karura Forest. Enjoy nature walks, waterfalls, and caves while surrounded by lush greenery.

Maasai Market

Join fellow members at exclusive events,
gatherings, and receptions. Connect with like-minded travelers and share your experiences.

Nairobi Railway Museum

Explore the history of Kenya's railways at the Nairobi Railway Museum. View vintage locomotives and carriages, offering a glimpse into the country's transportation heritage.

Nairobi National Museum

Discover Kenya's rich cultural and natural heritage at the Nairobi National Museum. Explore exhibits on art, paleontology, ethnography, and archaeology.

Bomas of Kenya

Experience traditional Kenyan music, dance,
and folklore at the Bomas
of Kenya cultural center.

Enjoy performances that showcase
the country's diverse ethnic groups.

Ngong Hills

For a day of outdoor adventure, consider a trek to Ngong Hills. Enjoy panoramic views of the Great Rift Valley and the surrounding landscapes. These excursions offer a mix of wildlife encounters, cultural experiences, and outdoor activities, making Nairobi a diverse and fascinating destination for visitors.
